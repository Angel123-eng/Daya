About us

Daya Care is a reliable and compassionate home nursing agency that offers top-notch nursing services to clients in the comfort of their own homes. Our team of highly skilled and experienced nurses is dedicated to providing personalized care and attention to each client we serve. We believe that staying at home is the best option for those who need medical care and support, and we are committed to making it possible by offering flexible schedules and cost-friendly service rates. At Daya Care, we understand the importance of maintaining the physical and emotional well-being of our clients, and we strive to provide the highest quality care possible to help them achieve optimal health and wellness.

OUR SERVICES

MEDICAL CARE SERVICE FOR ELDERLY PEOPLE

Our health care package is designed specifically to cater to the unique health care needs of senior citizens. We provide a comprehensive and integrated approach to managing their health and wellbeing. Our team of experts comprises of highly qualified and experienced doctors, nurses, and care managers, who work together to track and monitor the seniors' health needs.

POST OPERATIVE CARE NURSING SUPPORT

Home Care Preferred has a team of professionals who are trained to provide post-operative support. They offer a range of assistance from helping with household chores to bathing, dressing, wound care and maintenance of catheter and colostomy bags. The team is dedicated to providing practical care aspects, allowing patients to spend quality time with their family and friends while recuperating.

24 HOURS AMBULANCE SERVICE

We are equipped to provide safe transport for critically ill patients.Call us and we provide the support to arrange ambulance from your location to home.Our services include arranging for an ambulance to transport patients from their current location to their home. We are able to transport both adult and pediatric patients in similar intensive care conditions from one hospital to another. Our ambulances are staffed with a qualified medical team, which includes a doctor, nursing staff, and paramedics. No matter what your transportation needs are, we are here to help ensure that the patient is transported safely and with proper medical attention.
